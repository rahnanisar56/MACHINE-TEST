India vs Pakistan, T20 World Cup 2026: IND defeat PAK by 61 runs at Colombo.

The Indian cricket team qualified for the Super 8 stage of the T20 World Cup 2026 with a 61-run victory over Pakistan in its third Group A match at the R Premadasa Cricket Stadium in Colombo, Sri Lanka, on Sunday. India rode on a sparkling 77 off just 40 deliveries from Ishan Kishan to post 175/7 in their 20 overs. Saim Ayub, with figures of 3/25, was the pick of the Pakistani bowlers.
