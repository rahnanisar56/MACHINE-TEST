Massive fire engulfs Jayalakshmi Silks in Kozhikode, no casualties.

A major fire broke out at a textile showroom in the city on Tuesday evening, triggering panic among shoppers and staff. The blaze erupted at the three-storey Jayalakshmi Silks building near the Kozhikode railway station aaround 6pm, when the premises were crowded with customers.

India vs Pakistan, T20 World Cup 2026: IND defeat PAK by 61 runs at Colombo.

The Indian cricket team qualified for the Super 8 stage of the T20 World Cup 2026 with a 61-run victory over Pakistan in its third Group A match at the R Premadasa Cricket Stadium in Colombo, Sri Lanka, on Sunday. India rode on a sparkling 77 off just 40 deliveries from Ishan Kishan to post 175/7 in their 20 overs. Saim Ayub, with figures of 3/25, was the pick of the Pakistani bowlers.

Kerala's Youngest Organ Donor: Ten-Month-Old Aalin Gifts Life to Four

In a moment of profound courage and compassion, ten-month-old Aalin Sherin Abraham from Mallappally, Pathanamthitta, became Kerala's youngest organ donor, giving new life to four individuals. The infant was declared brain dead at Amrita Hospital after sustaining critical injuries in a road accident on February 5 near Pallam Borma Junction on M.C. Road.
